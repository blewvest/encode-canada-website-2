We make AI
a big deal
EJC has three main pillars:

Content

We create educational content about AI: the basics, the ethics, and the academic. This content ranges from Instagram infographics about tech to full-length survey reports about key ethical issues with AI applications. All our content is accessible to people with or without technical or policy backgrounds.

Connections

We foster connections between students, industry professionals, academic researchers, and policymakers. Through hosting and attending workshops, symposiums, and other events, we hope to encourage an interdisciplinary understanding of AI and elevate the voice of youth in technological innovation and application.

Community

We plan to create a network of organizations across Canada that can share resources to create and push for our common goal: AI that works for people. We call these organizations “nodes”, alluding to their unique characteristics and functions but their essential role in the larger Canadian AI ecosystem.
